Nostalgia

By Martina Barrera-Hernandez

back then,
no rivers seemed so,

wide
no stone walls so,
daunting

rivers?
just puddles
and
walls?
just piles of rocks

cardboard boxes
weren’t just
flimsy combinations of paper,
but
castles and ships
rockets and fortresses

a stick
wasn’t a abandoned tree limb,
but
a sword
or wand

back then,
your mind wasn’t
constrained
contained
confined

take me back
